Registration and Account Management
 Previous  Next
Registering with easycreate.com is easy and fast. Go to Gosites SignUp Page and provide the very basic information. Registration is FREE and valid for life time.

After you have signed up with the site, if you want to change your profile or password or change your easycreate.com site style or view your Payment Details so far, you can find the details of doing it in the next few sections.

Click on the 'Profile Manager' in the main menu or 'Profile Manager' link in the bottom menu to get the options for managing your profile.
  • Edit Profile

In the Profile Manager, click on 'Edit Profile' icon. Edit your name, addresses, email, phone and FAX etc in the following page.

  • Change Password

Click on 'Change Password' icon in the Profile Manager. Enter your new password and confirm it. Click on 'Save' to update your password.
  • Edit Stylesheet

The stylesheet defines the color scheme of easycreate.com site for you. Thus you can select your favorite color scheme by changing the stylesheet. Click on 'Edit StyleSheet' icon in the Profile Manager. Select a style and save. The site color scheme changes instantaneously.
  • Payment Details

The details of payment done by you so far for publishing your sites can be viewed by going to Payment Details in the Integration Manager.
