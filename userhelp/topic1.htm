What is iScripts EasyCreate 3.0?
   Next

iScripts EasyCreate 3.0 is a website builder that contains a wide range of templates and options for any novice or advanced user to quickly create and publish professional websites. You can select from a large list of high quality templates for any theme or occasion and create youe sites in minutes, regardless of your knowledge in HTML. The EasyCreate advanced website creator is designed for the people who want to create and manage websites quickly at a great price.

iScripts EasyCreate 3.0 comes equipped with a large amount of features, making usability easy.

The main features are:

  • Multiple Templates and Categories
  • Instant Site Building
  • Instant Site Editing
  • Custom Logo and Image Capability
  • Image Gallery
  • Online Image Editing
  • Custom Forms
  • Guest Book and Feedback Integration
  • Site Promotion Tools
  • Direct FTP - Upload files to your site using the site builder FTP.
  • Simple and Advanced Editors - Ideal for experienced or novice users.
  • Search Engine Optimization Tools
  • File Management - Using the site builder FTP.
